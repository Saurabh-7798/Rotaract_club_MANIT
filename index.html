About Us

Charity does wonders for our emotional well-being. These offerings are not necessarily in the form of money, but rather more precious attribute, that is your time for community services. Social work is seen as the duty of only NGOs. MANIT students are contributing to the society under the mission of ROTARACT CLUB MANIT that does charity work like water to wing, career counselling, collection and distribution drive, teaching the children in slums, NGO visits, celebrating festivals in orphanages and many others. This pandemic and imposed restrictions did not hassle their sturdy intentions of giving and caring. It is the responsibility of the whole society; everyone should contribute as per their capabilities. This will help reduce the gap between underprivileged and privileged which will lead to a strong community and ultimately a prosperous nation.
